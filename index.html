About NeuroAssist

NeuroAssist is a thoughtfully designed cognitive support application created specifically for individuals experiencing memory challenges due to Alzheimer's disease. Our mission is to provide a gentle, intuitive tool that helps maintain daily routines, strengthen cognitive abilities, and preserve precious memories.

We understand the unique challenges faced by both individuals with Alzheimer's and their caregivers. That's why NeuroAssist combines practical daily support with memory-enhancing activities in a simple, accessible interface.
